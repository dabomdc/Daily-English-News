Winter Lake Fun!
It is winter now.
The lake is frozen and hard like ice.
Little ducks cannot swim in the frozen lake.
They walk on the ice and look for food.
문장 버튼을 누르면 1문장씩 읽어줍니다.
WORDS (5)
Winter겨울 · The cold season.
Frozen얼어붙은 · When water turns to ice.
Lake호수 · A big area of water.
Swim수영하다 · To move in water.
Walk걷다 · To move on your feet.
